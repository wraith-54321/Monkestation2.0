handle_teleport_conditionsHandles qualifiers for enabling teleportation of boulders. Returns TRUE if the teleportation can proceed, FALSE otherwise.
pre_collect_boulderBegins to collect a boulder from the available boulders list in SSore_generation. The actual movement is then handled by collect_boulder() after a timed callback.
