bodycam_upgrade
/obj/item/bodycam_upgrade

Bodycamera Upgrade

An item that can be inserted into any piece of clothing that goes on the Exosuit slot. Allows you to turn it on/off with an ID card to add the suit & the wearer's name into the security camera system.

Proc Details

proc
install_camera
(/obj/item/installing_into, /mob/user)

Installs the bodycamera into a piece of clothing, updating the overlays on the mob if they're actively wearing it.

proc
on_attackby
(/datum/source, /obj/item/item, /mob/living/user)

On Attackby

Called when the piece of clothing the bodycamera is installed into is attacked by an item. If the item is an ID card, it will turn the camera on/off.

proc
on_checked_overlays
(/obj/item/source, /list/overlays, /mutable_appearance/standing, isinhands, icon_file)

On checked overlays

Called when the item the bodycamera is installed into is getting their worn overlays updated. We add our body camera overlay to the list of overlays.

proc
on_emp_act
(/atom/source, severity, protection)

on_emp_act

Called when the body camera is EMPed. Will destroy the camera, regardless of whether it is equipped or not.

proc
on_examine_more
(/atom/source, /mob/user, /list/examine_list)

Examine more

Called when the item the bodycamera is installed into is double-examined, letting it know it's examined and how to get it out.

proc
on_screwdriver_act
(/atom/source, /mob/user, /obj/item/tool)

Screwdriver act

Called when a screwdriver is used on the item the bodycamera is installed into. Removes the bodycamera from the clothing and puts it in the user's hand.

proc
turn_off
(/mob/user)

Turns the camera off. Will be silent if 'user' is null.

proc
turn_on
(/mob/living/user, /obj/item/card/id/id_card)

Turns the camera on. Will be silent if 'user' is null, but it REQUIRES either a user or a provided ID. Because cameras are named after the ID, or person if there isn't one, then having neither means we can't turn on at all.

proc
uninstall_camera
(/obj/item/taking_from, /mob/user)

Uninstalls the bodycamera from a piece of clothing.
