devil
/datum/antagonist/devil

The devil, an agent of hell that listens only to himself and upper devils

Basic gameplay: Go up to people with custom-made contracts and scam them out of their soul

Do note contracts the devil makes are "sticky", in a way that most abilities will transfer bodies. If you make a deal with the devil, better expect that blindness to still affect you whilst in a borg body.
